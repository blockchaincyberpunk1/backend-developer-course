Authentication Mechanisms

Course: Backend Developer Course

Assignment Deadline: [Insert Deadline]

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, you will delve into the world of authentication mechanisms in web development. The objective is to explore different authentication methods and understand their significance in securing web applications. You will define user authentication, explain its importance, and then research and describe at least three common authentication mechanisms, discussing their use cases and security considerations.

Instructions:
Please complete the following tasks to explore authentication mechanisms:

Task 1: User Authentication Definition (20 points)
Define what user authentication is and explain its significance in securing web applications. Discuss the role of authentication in verifying the identity of users and controlling access to resources.

Task 2: Authentication Mechanisms (60 points)
Research and describe at least three common authentication mechanisms used in web development. Your description for each mechanism should include:

An overview of the authentication mechanism and how it works.
Use cases or scenarios where this authentication mechanism is commonly applied.
Security considerations or best practices associated with the mechanism.
You can choose from a variety of authentication mechanisms, such as:

Username and password authentication.
OAuth authentication.
JWT (JSON Web Tokens) authentication.
SAML (Security Assertion Markup Language) authentication.
Two-factor authentication (2FA).
Social media authentication (e.g., Sign in with Google, Facebook).
Task 3: Presentation (10 points)
Organize your findings into a clear and well-structured document. Ensure that your assignment is free of grammatical and spelling errors.

Submission Guidelines:

Compile your research and explanations into a document (PDF or Word).
Include any relevant references or sources you consulted during your research.
Submit your assignment as a document along with any necessary supplementary files.
Grading Rubric:
Your assignment will be graded based on the following criteria:
